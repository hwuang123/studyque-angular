Vision

Students of all ages can quickly forget about when or what lessons are due or what day theirexams fall on. We believe that it is imperative to have a program that serves as a scheduler which will provide you complete control where you can have text messages sent to cell phones, an email sent to various email addresses or an automated voice message that can call any phone (cell, home, work). This instrument would make it almost impossible to forget educational related tasks and avoids those late, cram night sessions performing last minute homework assignments.

Imagine if you have a term paper due a month from now or there's a math test slated for next week. Once registered for StudyCue you create your calendar associated entry and from there setup a reminder. This can afford a student the necessary time for study preparation where the system reminds you every day, once a week or every other week, whatever you deem fit for your specific situation.

Try StudyCue today and reap the many benefits that this application can supply to reach academic success.
